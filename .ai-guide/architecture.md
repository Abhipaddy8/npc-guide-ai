# Architecture
**Project**: npc-guide-ai
**Language**: TypeScript
**Extras**: OpenAI
**Complexity**: complex
## Features
- Design a compelling README that highlights the tool's value proposition.
- Develop an onboarding experience that showcases the tool's capabilities within the first 30 seconds.
- Implement a mechanism for users to easily share their experiences and results on social media.
- Create a community forum or platform for users to share tips, feedback, and collaborate.
- Design CLI output that is visually appealing and easily shareable.
- Implement a system for users to track their progress and see the value of the tool over time.
- Explore open-source options to encourage community contributions and adoption.
- Identify potential paid features that offer additional value to users.
- Develop a strategy for organic discovery and word-of-mouth marketing.
## Constraints
- Avoid intrusive or overly aggressive marketing tactics.
- Ensure that any lock-in mechanisms are fair and provide genuine value to users.
